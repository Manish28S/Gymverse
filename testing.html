About Me

Gym Trainer

Gymverse was founded in October 2022 by Manish Shahu & Mayuresh Kharkate. They created the fundamental training methodology & shared it through his free book “Get Shredded” to prove that anyone can get fit with the knowledge of Quantified Nutrition. The fitness mantra underpinning Gymverse is to educate and empower the end-user to transform & inspire others to get fit. The Gymverse serves as a one-stop solution for all health & fitness guidance and related needs. Through its freemium model, Gymverse offers free access to a host of industry-standard diet & training tools, a number of other cutting-edge features, and a community of fitness experts & enthusiasts. Users can get personalised guidance, customised plans & weekly follow-ups with Gymverse's certified coaches by paying a small premium. At Gymverse, we provide a safe space that is free of judgement. Till date, we have helped transform the health and lifestyle of more than 300,000 people from across the globe and from all walks of life. We welcome you to become a part of this fitness revolution.
